Established in 1935 by Pandit Hiralal Shastri and Smt. Ratan Shastri, Banasthali Vidyapith is a deemed to be university in Rajasthan. It is a woman-only university and has been awarded ‘A++’ Grade by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The university focuses on a five-fold education system, i.e. physical, aesthetical, practical, intellectual and morality. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in multi-disciplines such as Education, Law, Home Science, ties, Social Sciences, Earth Sciences, Management Studies, Mathematics & Computing, Life Sciences, Automation, Physical Sciences and Fine Arts.
